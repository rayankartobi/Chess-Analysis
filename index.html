The Chess Analyzer is a powerful tool powered by the Stockfish engine — one of the strongest and most widely used chess engines in the world. To use it, simply paste the PGN (Portable Game Notation) file of your played game, and the bot will thoroughly analyze every move made throughout the match. Each move is then categorized and color-coded based on its quality: Brilliant moves are highlighted in cyan, Great moves in violet-blue, Best moves and Excellent moves in green, and Good moves in light green. Moves that follow well-known opening theory are marked as Book moves in light brown. On the other end of the spectrum, Inaccuracies are flagged in yellow, Mistakes in light orange, Misses in light red, and Blunders — the most costly errors — are marked in bold red. This color-coded breakdown gives players a clear and intuitive visual overview of their performance, helping them identify strengths, spot critical errors, and continuously improve their game.
